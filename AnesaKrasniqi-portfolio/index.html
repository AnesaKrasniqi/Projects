HI THERE!

I'm Anesa Krasniqi

My interest in computer science began around five years ago when I first came into contact with computer coding in school while creating a small website. From there on, I was captivated. For me, working on computer code is my way of being creative, since I find it difficult to be creative in more common ways such as drawing. Every time I write a piece of computer code, I get the feeling I have created a piece of art and reading computer code, to me, feels like looking at a piece of art.
